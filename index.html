Welcome! I am Assistant Professor of Economics at Hitotsubashi University.

My research focuses broadly on labor economics. I am particularly interested in human capital dynamics over the life course.

I am a co-organizer of Industry and Labor Economics Workshop. Please let me know whenever you would like to visit us and present your research in the workshop.
