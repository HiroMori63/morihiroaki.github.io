Welcome! I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at Hitotsubashi University.

My research focuses broadly on labor economics. I am particularly interested in human capital dynamics over the life course.

I co-organize Industry and Labor Workshop. Please let me know if you'd like to visit us and present your research in the workshop.
